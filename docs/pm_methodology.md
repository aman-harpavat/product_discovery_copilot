PM Methodology

Core Principle

Research questions drive analysis.

Analysis drives opportunities.

Opportunities drive interview planning.

Do not jump directly from clusters to opportunities.

⸻

Segmentation Framework

Segments must emerge from evidence.

Do not create decorative personas.

For each segment include:

* segment_name
* description
* estimated_record_count
* percentage_of_relevant_records
* source_distribution
* supporting_cluster_ids
* representative_quotes
* primary_JTBDs
* top_pain_points
* confidence_level
* confidence_rationale

Possible segmentation dimensions:

* intent
* behavior
* engagement level
* pain patterns
* workaround behavior

⸻

JTBD Framework

Identify:

* Functional Jobs
* Emotional Jobs
* Social Jobs

Support all JTBDs with evidence.

Do not invent JTBDs.

⸻

Needs Framework

Extract needs from:

JTBDs + pain points + workarounds

Needs should be solution-agnostic.

⸻

Pain Point Framework

For every pain point include:

* frequency
* severity
* associated JTBD
* supporting evidence
* representative quote

⸻

Workaround Framework

Track:

* workaround
* frequency
* effort required
* associated pain point
* associated JTBD

Strong workarounds indicate strong opportunity signals.

⸻

Opportunity Framework

Do not treat complaints as opportunities.

Convert:

Needs + Pain Points → Opportunity Statements

Every opportunity should include:

* description
* supporting clusters
* supporting research questions
* supporting JTBDs
* supporting segments
* representative evidence

⸻

Opportunity Ranking Framework

Evaluate:

Opportunity Strength

* Frequency
* Severity
* JTBD Importance
* Opportunity Gap
* Workaround Strength

Solution Potential

* AI Leverage
* Segment Coverage

Brief Alignment

* Research Question Coverage
* Success Criteria Impact
* Scope Relevance

All opportunities remain visible.

Ranking is recommendation, not filtering.

⸻

Opportunity Traceability

Every opportunity must map to:

* research questions
* success criteria
* clusters
* JTBDs
* segments

Show evidence chain.

⸻

Success Criteria Impact

For each opportunity assess:

* impact on each success criterion
* rationale
* supporting evidence

Do not hardcode Spotify-specific outcomes.

Use the success criteria locked in the brief.

⸻

Confidence Framework

Apply confidence only to:

* segments
* JTBDs
* needs
* pain points
* opportunities

Do not apply confidence to individual feedback items.

Confidence drivers:

* evidence volume
* source coverage
* evidence depth
* consistency
* recency
* caveats

Use:

* High
* Medium
* Low

⸻

Research Question Coverage

Every report must explicitly answer:

* each research question
* with evidence
* with metrics
* with quotes
* with limitations

Do not merely summarize clusters.

⸻

Interview Planning Framework

Generate briefs only for recommended opportunities.

Use recommendation language.

Say:

* Suggested recruitment criteria
* Suggested interview areas

Do not use directive language.

Interview segment should come from the primary affected segment for the selected opportunity.

⸻

Repeat Listening / Core Outcome Analysis

When relevant to the brief:

Analyze:

* failure causes
* user behavior patterns
* workarounds
* unmet needs
* affected segments
* opportunity implications

Use:

* cluster evidence
* metrics
* quotes
* success criteria
* research question coverage

Never overstate evidence.
